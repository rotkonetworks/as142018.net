Infrastructure Builders' Programme

The anycast ranges are allocated to the Infrastructure Builders' Programme (IBP),
a community initiative providing decentralized infrastructure services for Polkadot networks.

IBP consists of independent micro-enterprises distributed globally, each operating from
different jurisdictions with diverse hardware and connectivity providers. Services are
delivered via GeoDNS and soon Anycast routing to ensure optimal performance and redundancy.
